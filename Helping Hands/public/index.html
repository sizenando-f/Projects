Inspiring Stories

The Power of a Smile

A volunteer in the Helping Hands program regularly visited a nursing home, taking the time to interact with the residents. An elderly gentleman, who used to be quiet and distant, received the volunteer's special attention. With his dedication and a simple smile, the volunteer managed to awaken the joy and enthusiasm of the man. The senior began to actively participate in activities, share stories, and became a lively presence in the nursing home community. This small gesture of affection and connection showed the transformative power of a smile.

Building a Bright Future

A young high school student was struggling academically and emotionally. She found support in the Helping Hands mentoring program, where she was matched with a mentor who inspired and encouraged her to persist in her studies. With the continuous support of her mentor, the student began to believe in her potential and to strive more and more. She overcame difficulties, improved her grades and got a scholarship to attend a prestigious university. This story shows how a dedicated mentor can help build a bright future for a young person.

Overcoming Barriers

A physically disabled woman had the dream of becoming a professional dancer, but many barriers seemed to stop her. She joined an inclusive dance group supported by Helping Hands, where she found a welcoming environment and support from peers and instructors. With hard work, determination, and the support of the community, she overcame her physical limitations and excelled as a dancer. Her story has inspired others with disabilities to pursue their dreams, showing that any obstacle can be overcome when you have passion and support.

FAQ

To register as a volunteer, you can click the "Register as a Volunteer" button on our website. Fill in the registration form with your personal information. After completing registration, you will be able to explore projects and apply for available volunteer opportunities.

No prior experience in volunteering is required. We value the diversity of skills and interests of our volunteers. There are projects suitable for beginners and others that require more specific skills. As you browse the projects, you'll find opportunities that suit your experience level.

Companies can register by clicking the "Register as a Company" button on our website. You will need to provide information about the company, its mission, areas of interest, ongoing projects and volunteering requirements. After registration, companies will have access to our volunteer base and will be able to publish projects in search of help.

After registering as a volunteer, you will be able to browse the projects available on our website. When you find a project that piques your interest, just click on "Apply" and fill out a short form indicating your interest in the project. The company will be notified of your application and will contact you if there is a match.

Volunteer responsibilities may vary according to the project and the needs of the company. Before committing to a project, it is important to review the information provided by the company to clearly understand the expectations regarding responsibilities, schedules and duration of volunteering. It is essential to fulfill the commitments assumed and to communicate transparently with the company.

Helping Hands is a platform dedicated to volunteering without financial remuneration. The projects offered are based on free volunteer work, where volunteers contribute their time and skills to social causes. However, volunteers have the opportunity to gain experience, establish valuable contacts and make a difference in their communities.

If you have any questions or need support, you can contact us through the "Contact" page on our website. Fill out the form with your information and message, and our support team will respond as soon as possible.
